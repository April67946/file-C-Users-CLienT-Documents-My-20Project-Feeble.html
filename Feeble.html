The Word “Feeble”

A Vocabulary and Meaning

Etymology

From Old French Febble", derived from latin flebilis meaning "weak" or "lamentable".

Definition

Feeble is a word means lacking physical strength, power, or effectiveness.

Denotation

In its literal sense, febble refers to:

  • Physical weakness
  • Lack of strength
Example (Denotation):
After the illness, he felt feeble and exhausted.

Connotation

Beyond its literal meaning, feeble often suggests:

  • Ineffectiveness
  • Weak argument or ideas
Example (Connotation):
Her explanation was feeble and unconvincing.
